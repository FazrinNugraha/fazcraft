## About The Project

**SocialCollateral AI** is a specialized Social Graph Engine developed to address a critical paradox in the microfinance industry: how to scale inclusive lending without losing the "social touch" that mitigates risk. As microfinance lenders like Amartha expand, they face an "asymmetry of information" where transaction data exists, yet the invisible dynamics of communal trust—the very foundation of the Grameen model—remain unquantified and "blind" to digital systems.

This project introduces **"Jaringan Amanah"**, a system that transforms these qualitative social assets into a quantitative **Group Trust Score**. By shifting the paradigm from individual credit scoring to graph-based risk assessment, we enable financial institutions to "see" the strength of social fabric and monetize the reputation of borrower groups.

The solution is architected around a **Social Graph Engine** that triangulates risk through **Three AI Lenses**:

1.  **Network Topology (Graph Analytics)**: Visualizing and computing the structural integrity of borrower clusters using centrality metrics to identify "toxic" versus "solid" groups.
2.  **Contextual Sentiment (NLP)**: Utilizing **Google Gemini** to decode unstructured field agent reports, detecting nuanced signals of conflict or cohesion that purely numerical data misses.
3.  **Physical Asset Valuation (Computer Vision)**: Deploying **Google Cloud Vision** to objectify the assessment of business and household assets, reducing human survey bias.

This integrated approach enables **Precision Lending** (reducing opportunity loss and bad debt) and **Operational Velocity** (automating pre-screening), effectively turning the "invisible" collateral of social trust into a bankable asset for the unbanked.
